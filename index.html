Welcome to Nightjar Rockery

Placeholder

Nightjar Rockery is six acres of deeply sloped rock and woodland approximately 1300 feet above sea level. We are situated within the Warwick Range. The former owners of this property created numerous rockery gardens amongst the rugged natural landscape, which we continue to cultivate and expand.

An important feature of this land is a steep, mossy woodland, which descends into a creek feeding Wigglesworth Lake. This area is part of an important wildlife corridor for large mammals and birds.

Our vision is to protect the wild character of this property, while growing flowers and vegetables with the guiding principle of environmental stewardship. This means that we never use pesticides or herbicides, and choose natural fertilisers if needed. We are also careful not to release any invasive species of plant.
